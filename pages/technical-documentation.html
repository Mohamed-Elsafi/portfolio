3D Structural Modeling

As a Senior Site Engineer with advanced technical skills, I utilize Tekla Structures software to create detailed 3D structural models for construction projects. These models serve as the foundation for precise shop drawings, accurate quantity calculations, and effective construction planning.

The 3D model shown here was created for the C-Ward Expansion Project at KFSH&RC, providing a comprehensive visualization of the steel structure with detailed specifications for each component. This approach allows for:

  • Precise coordination between structural elements
  • Early detection of potential clashes or design issues
  • Accurate material quantity takeoffs
  • Clear communication with fabricators and installation teams
  • Streamlined construction sequencing

Quantity Calculations

A critical aspect of my role as a Senior Site Engineer involves precise quantity calculations for excavation, backfilling, concrete, and other construction materials. These calculations ensure accurate budgeting, efficient resource allocation, and proper construction planning.

The examples shown here demonstrate my methodical approach to quantity takeoffs, with detailed measurements and calculations for the C-Ward Expansion Project. My expertise includes:

  • Detailed volume calculations for excavation and backfilling
  • Area-based quantity takeoffs with precise measurements
  • Material quantity estimations based on structural designs
  • Deduction calculations for accurate net quantities
  • Comprehensive documentation for client approval and contractor reference
